The Story Behind the Name

A word
in Armenian.

My father had a word for me. An Armenian word — a little untranslatable — for the kind of curiosity that drives a father a little crazy and fills his heart completely. He softened the ending, made it feminine, made it his. He called me Antanela.

In his eyes I was beautiful even getting older. Sometimes he would ask, laughing, what was my secret.

Antanela believes that nature holds everything skin needs — that gently protecting and nourishing it translates into lasting beauty. For years formulas were quietly developed for family and close ones, guided by a deep understanding of how beauty and healthy living are inseparable.

Those formulas found their way to one of the world's finest cosmetic laboratories in Milan, Italy — where European standards of purity and safety are among the strictest.

Curiosity and care, pressed into the jars.

Key Ingredients

i.

Chamomile Flower Water

Where most creams begin with plain water, Éclat starts with a soothing chamomile infusion — calming, anti-inflammatory, and gently preparing skin from the very first layer.

ii.

Squalane

Plant-derived and weightless, Squalane mirrors your skin's own natural lipids — delivering deep hydration without greasiness, perfectly absorbed.

iii.

Cupuaçu, Shea & Mango Butter

Three rare botanical butters — Cupuaçu from the Amazon, Shea, and Mango Seed — each delivering a different dimension of nourishment and lasting softness.

iv.

Astaxanthin

One of nature's most potent antioxidants — working alongside Pomegranate Bark and Coffee Seed Extract to defend, brighten and support lasting radiance.

v.

Apricot Kernel & Sunflower Oil

Lightweight botanical oils rich in vitamins — absorbing beautifully to leave skin soft, balanced and naturally luminous.

vi.

Yarrow Extract

An ancient healing plant — anti-inflammatory and skin-balancing, quietly working to support equilibrium and calm.

The Daily Ritual

How to use Éclat

Morning and evening, as part of your daily skincare ritual

01

Cleanse

Begin with clean, dry skin — your canvas for everything that follows.

02

Take

Take a small amount of Éclat and warm it gently between your fingertips.

03

Apply

Gently distribute across face and neck with upward, lifting movements.

04

Absorb

Massage until fully absorbed. Follow with sunscreen in the morning.

Combination skin: apply lightly on the T-zone. Sensitive skin: patch test recommended.
